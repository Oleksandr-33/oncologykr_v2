# CONTENT-STRATEGY.md - Content Plan

## Overview

This document outlines the content strategy for oncologykr, ensuring all content is accurate, accessible, trustworthy, and serves the needs of patients, families, and healthcare providers.

---

## Content Vision

**"Empowering patients and families with clear, accurate, and compassionate oncology information that helps them navigate their cancer journey with confidence."**

---

## Content Principles

### 1. Accuracy Above All
Every piece of medical information must be verified by licensed oncology professionals. No exceptions. Inaccurate medical information can cause harm.

### 2. Plain Language
Write at an 8th-grade reading level. Explain medical terms when used. Use short sentences. Avoid jargon.

### 3. Patient-Centered
Write from the patient's perspective. Answer the questions they're actually asking. Anticipate their concerns.

### 4. Actionable
Every content piece should give users a clear next step. Don't leave them wondering what to do.

### 5. Current
Outdated information is dangerous in healthcare. Content must be reviewed regularly and updated when policies change.

### 6. Compassionate
Remember that readers may be frightened, grieving, or overwhelmed. Write with warmth and empathy while remaining professional.

### Medical Reviewer Responsibilities

1. Verify all medical facts against current guidelines
2. Check for outdated information
3. Ensure appropriate disclaimers present
4. Confirm no misleading claims
5. Validate treatment information accuracy
6. Review for patient safety concerns
7. Sign off with credentials displayed

### Required Disclaimers

**For all medical content:**
```
Ця інформація призначена лише для освітніх цілей і не замінює
професійну медичну консультацію, діагностику чи лікування.
Завжди консультуйтеся зі своїм лікарем щодо будь-яких питань
про ваш стан здоров'я.

---

This information is for educational purposes only and does not
replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Always consult your doctor about any questions regarding your
health condition.
```

**For symptom information:**
```
Якщо у вас є тривожні симптоми, негайно зверніться до лікаря.
Не відкладайте звернення за медичною допомогою.

---

If you have concerning symptoms, contact your doctor immediately.
Do not delay seeking medical care.
```

### Sources and Citations

- Cite authoritative sources (national guidelines, major cancer organizations)
- Include publication dates for cited research
- Link to original sources when appropriate
- Update when guidelines change

### Prohibited Claims

- Cure guarantees
- Unproven treatments
- Testimonials implying guaranteed outcomes
- Comparisons disparaging other providers
- Misleading statistics
- Fear-based messaging

### Writing Guidelines

**Readability:**
- Sentences: 15-20 words average
- Paragraphs: 3-5 sentences maximum
- Reading level: 8th grade (Flesch-Kincaid)
- Use bullet points for lists
- Use numbered steps for procedures

**Terminology:**
- Define medical terms on first use
- Provide pronunciation guides for complex terms
- Link to glossary entries
- Use Ukrainian medical terminology with explanations

**Formatting:**
- Clear heading hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- Bold key terms and important warnings
- Use tables for comparisons
- Include relevant images with alt text

### Bio Writing Guidelines

```

3. Experience highlights (1-2 sentences)
```
